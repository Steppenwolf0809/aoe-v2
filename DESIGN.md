# Design System: AOE v2 (Clean LegalTech)

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
**Vibe:** Professional, Trustworthy, Modern, "Clean Legal".
**Aesthetic:** Airy layouts, distinct hierarchy, soft shadows, and precise typography.
**Density:** Comfortable, with generous whitespace.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Brand Blue Primary (#024089)**: Main brand color for primary buttons, active states, and key highlights. Extracted directly from the official logo. Represents authority and trust.
- **Deep Slate (#0f172a)**: Primary text and headings. Strong contrast against white.
- **Cool Gray (#64748b)**: Secondary text, descriptions, and subtle borders.
- **Pure White (#ffffff)**: Main background.
- **Soft Cloud (#f8fafc)**: Secondary background for cards and sections.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Family:** "Inter" (Clean, modern sans-serif).
- **Headings:** Bold weights, tight tracking.
- **Body:** Regular weight, readable line-height (1.6).

## 4. Component Stylings
- **Buttons:**
    - Primary: Solid Brand Blue (#024089), White text, Rounded-12px (medium rounded), subtle shadow.
    - Secondary: White background, Brand Blue border, Brand Blue text.
- **Cards:**
    - Background: White (#ffffff).
    - Shadow: Soft, diffused shadow (`box-shadow: 0 4px 6px -1px rgb(0 0 0 / 0.05)`).
    - Borders: Very subtle gray border or none.
    - Roundness: 12px (`rounded-xl` in Tailwind).
- **Inputs:**
    - Background: White or very light gray.
    - Border: Gray-200, focus ring in Brand Blue (#024089).
    - Roundness: 12px.

## 5. Layout Principles
- **Container:** Centered max-width containers.
- **Spacing:** Consistent 8px grid (16px, 24px, 32px, 48px).
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned text for readability, centered headings for impact.
