A comprehensive and clean "Services" page for AOE v2.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
**Vibe:** Professional, Trustworthy, Modern, "Clean Legal".
**Aesthetic:** Airy layouts, distinct hierarchy, soft shadows, and precise typography.
**Density:** Comfortable, with generous whitespace.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Brand Blue Primary (#024089)**: Main brand color for primary buttons, active states, and key highlights. Represents authority and trust.
- **Deep Slate (#0f172a)**: Primary text and headings. Strong contrast against white.
- **Cool Gray (#64748b)**: Secondary text, descriptions, and subtle borders.
- **Pure White (#ffffff)**: Main background.
- **Soft Cloud (#f8fafc)**: Secondary background for cards and sections.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Family:** "Inter" (Clean, modern sans-serif).
- **Headings:** Bold weights, tight tracking.
- **Body:** Regular weight, readable line-height (1.6).

## 4. Component Stylings
- **Buttons:**
    - Primary: Solid Emerald (#10b77f), White text, Rounded-12px (medium rounded), subtle shadow.
    - Secondary: White background, Emerald border, Emerald text.
- **Cards:**
    - Background: White (#ffffff).
    - Shadow: Soft, diffused shadow (`box-shadow: 0 4px 6px -1px rgb(0 0 0 / 0.05)`).
    - Borders: Very subtle gray border or none.
    - Roundness: 12px (`rounded-xl` in Tailwind).
- **Inputs:**
    - Background: White or very light gray.
    - Border: Gray-200, focus ring in Emerald (#10b77f).
    - Roundness: 12px.

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header:** Minimal navigation with Logo and "Sign In".
2. **Hero Section:** Centered headline "All Your Legal Tools in One Place". Subtext: "Select a service to begin your automated process."
3. **Services Grid:** 3-column layout.
    - **Card 1:** "Notary Calculator". Icon: Calculator. Action: "Calculate Fees" (Secondary Button).
    - **Card 2:** "Vehicle Purchase". Icon: Car. Action: "Create Contract" (Secondary Button).
    - **Card 3:** "Power of Attorney". Icon: Pen. Action: "Draft Now" (Secondary Button).
    - **Card 4:** "Divorce by Mutual Consent". Icon: User group slash. Action: "Start Process" (Secondary Button).
    - **Card 5:** "Lease Agreement". Icon: Home. Action: "Create Contract" (Secondary Button).
    - **Card 6:** "Exit Permit". Icon: Airplane. Action: "Generate Permit" (Secondary Button).
4. **Footer:** Simple links, copyright.
